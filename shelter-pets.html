About Paws & Hearts Animal Shelter

Paws & Hearts Animal Shelter is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned, abused, and neglected animals in our community. Founded in 2005, we have helped over 10,000 animals find their forever homes.

Our mission is to provide a safe haven for animals in need, promote responsible pet ownership through education, and work towards ending pet overpopulation through spay/neuter programs.

No-Kill Shelter

We never euthanize for space or time constraints.

Full Veterinary Care

All animals receive complete medical care before adoption.

Volunteer Powered

Our dedicated volunteers make our work possible.

Our Facility

Our shelter features:

  • Spacious indoor/outdoor dog runs
  • Free-roaming cat rooms with climbing structures
  • Separate areas for small animals
  • On-site veterinary clinic
  • Training and socialization areas
  • Meet-and-greet rooms for potential adopters

Our Team

Our shelter is staffed by a dedicated team of animal care professionals, veterinarians, and volunteers who are passionate about animal welfare. Our staff undergoes regular training to ensure they provide the best possible care for our animals.

Our Adoption Process

At Paws & Hearts Animal Shelter, we're committed to finding the perfect match between our animals and their new families. Our adoption process is designed to ensure that both you and your new pet will be happy together for years to come.

1

Browse Available Pets

Start by browsing our available pets online or visiting our shelter in person. Our staff can help you find animals that match your lifestyle and preferences.

2

Submit an Adoption Application

Once you've found a pet you're interested in, complete our adoption application. This helps us understand your living situation, experience with pets, and expectations.

Adoption Application
3

Meet Your Potential Pet

Schedule a meet-and-greet with the animal you're interested in. This is a great opportunity to see if you and the pet are a good match. We encourage all household members to attend this meeting.

4

Home Check (if applicable)

For some animals, especially those with special needs, we may conduct a brief home check to ensure your living environment is suitable for the specific pet.

5

Adoption Fee and Contract

If approved, you'll pay the adoption fee and sign our adoption contract. The fee helps cover the cost of vaccinations, spay/neuter surgery, microchipping, and other care the animal received while at our shelter.

6

Take Your New Pet Home!

Congratulations! You can now take your new family member home. We'll provide you with information about your pet's medical history, dietary needs, and behavior, as well as tips for a smooth transition.

Post-Adoption Support

Our commitment to you and your pet doesn't end at adoption. We offer post-adoption support, including behavior advice, training resources, and medical guidance. Don't hesitate to contact us if you have any questions or concerns after bringing your new pet home.

Adoption Fees

Our adoption fees help cover the cost of care each animal receives, including:

  • Veterinary examination
  • Vaccinations
  • Spay/neuter surgery
  • Microchipping
  • Deworming and flea treatment
  • Any necessary medical treatment
Animal Fee
Dogs (1+ years) $200
Puppies (under 1 year) $250
Cats (1+ years) $125
Kittens (under 1 year) $150
Small Animals $50-$75

*Senior citizens (65+) and military personnel receive a 20% discount on adoption fees.

Adoption Requirements

  • Must be at least 21 years old
  • Valid ID with current address
  • Proof of residence (if renting, landlord approval)
  • All household members must agree to adoption
  • Time to spend with your new pet
