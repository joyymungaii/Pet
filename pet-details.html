Meet Buddy

Buddy is a friendly and energetic 2-year-old Golden Retriever who loves to play fetch and go for walks. He was surrendered to our rescue when his previous family had to move to a place that didn't allow pets.

Buddy is great with people of all ages and gets along well with other dogs. He's house-trained, knows basic commands like sit, stay, and come, and walks well on a leash. He's a smart boy who is eager to please and would benefit from continued training.

He has a lot of energy and would do best in a home with a yard where he can run and play. Buddy loves water and would be thrilled to have a family that takes him swimming occasionally. He's a loyal companion who will bring joy and love to his forever home.

Adoption Process

Interested in adopting Buddy? Here's what you need to know about our adoption process:

  1. Submit an adoption application (online or in person)
  2. Application review by our adoption team (1-3 days)
  3. Phone interview with a potential adopter
  4. Home visit to ensure the environment is suitable
  5. Meet and greet with Buddy (multiple visits may be recommended)
  6. Adoption fee payment and contract signing
  7. Welcome Buddy to his new home!
Adoption Fee

The adoption fee for Buddy is $350. This fee helps cover the cost of his care, including:

  • Neutering
  • Microchipping
  • Vaccinations
  • Heartworm testing and preventatives
  • General medical care
Adoption Requirements
  • Must be at least 21 years old
  • Proof of residence (own or rent with pet permission)
  • Valid ID
  • Veterinary references (if you have or had other pets)
  • Home environment suitable for an active dog
  • Commitment to providing proper care, training, and love
